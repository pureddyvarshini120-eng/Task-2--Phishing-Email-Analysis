 Task 2: Phishing Email Analysis

Objective
Identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email sample.

 Sample Email

Sender: support-paypal@secure-account-verify.com

Subject: Urgent! Your PayPal Account Will Be Suspended

Body:

Dear Customer,

We detected unusual activity on your account.
Verify your account within 24 hours or it will be suspended.

Click here:
http://paypal-account-verify-login.com

Thank you,
PayPal Security Team

Phishing Indicators Found

1. Fake sender email address
2. Suspicious URL
3. Urgent language
4. Generic greeting
5. Spoofing attempt
6. Social engineering tactics

Tools Used

- Email analysis
- Browser URL inspection

Conclusion

The email is a phishing attempt designed to steal user credentials by creating urgency and impersonating a trusted organization.
